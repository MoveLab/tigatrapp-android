About Mosquito Alert

This project has been made possible thanks to the growing support of a number of public and private entities, as well as many professionals.

The project is coordinated by the ICREA Movement Ecology Laboratory at CEAB-CSIC and CREAF.

The project is funded by the “la Caixa” Banking Foundation.

The project is co-financed by the Independent Public Health Body of Girona Provincial Council (Dipsalut), the Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness, the Spanish Foundation for Science and Technology (FECYT) and the private company lokímica.

Many of the photographs in the Mosquito Alert app have been donated by R. Eritja.
