About Tigatrapp

AtrapaelTigre.com is a citizen science project coordinated by the ICREA-Movement Ecology Lab at the Centre for Advanced Studies of Blanes (CSIC).

The project is being implemented in collaboration with CSIC scientific communication officers (Associate Vice President for Scientific Culture, Balearic Islands Delegation, Catalonia Delegation, and Valencia Delegation) and the University of Murcia.

The project is co-financed by the Spanish Foundation for Science and Technology (FECYT) and the Recercaixa research grant program within the framework of the project "Citizen Science: education and research". Bloom is collaborating in the fight against the tiger mosquito by also sponsoring the AtrapaelTigre.com project.

The project also has the support of several other public and private institutions.

All of the photos used in Tigatrapp were provided by R. Eritja.
